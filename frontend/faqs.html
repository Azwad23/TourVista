Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about TourVista

General

What is TourVista?

TourVista is a centralized group tour and event management platform. We organize tours, treks, cycling trips, and outings across Bangladesh and beyond. Users can browse events, register, pay online, and manage everything from one place.

Is it free to create an account?

Yes! Creating a TourVista account is completely free. You only pay when you register for a specific event.

Can I sign up with Google or GitHub?

Absolutely! We support one-click sign-up via Google and GitHub for your convenience. You can link these accounts during registration or from the login page.

Events & Registration

How do I register for an event?

Browse the Events page, click on an event you like, then click "Register Now". You'll be asked to complete payment via bKash or Nagad. Once paid, your registration status will be Pending until approved by an admin.

What do the registration statuses mean?

Pending — Awaiting admin review.
Approved — You're confirmed for the event!
Rejected — Not approved; contact support for details.
Waitlisted — Event is full; you'll be notified if a spot opens.

Can I cancel my registration?

To cancel a registration, please contact us at support@tourvista.com with your event name and registration details. Cancellation policies may vary by event.

What is the wishlist?

The wishlist lets you save events you're interested in for later. Click the heart icon on any event card to add it. View all your saved events on the Wishlist page.

Payments

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bKash and Nagad — the two most popular mobile payment platforms in Bangladesh. You'll be redirected to the payment gateway during registration.

How do I request a refund?

Email support@tourvista.com with your registration details, payment transaction ID, and reason for the refund. We'll process eligible refunds within 5-7 business days.

My payment failed but money was deducted. What do I do?

Don't worry! If the payment didn't complete on our end, the amount will be auto-refunded by bKash/Nagad within 24-72 hours. If it doesn't, email us at support@tourvista.com with your transaction details.

Account

How do I update my profile?

Go to the Profile page where you can edit your name, phone number, and upload a profile picture. Phone numbers must follow the format: +880 followed by 10 digits.

How do I change my password?

On the Profile page, scroll to the "Change Password" section. Enter your current password and your new password to update it.

Can I change my email address?

For security reasons, email addresses cannot be changed directly. If you need to update your email, please contact support@tourvista.com for assistance.

Didn't find your answer?

Our team is always ready to help. Reach out and we'll respond as soon as possible.
