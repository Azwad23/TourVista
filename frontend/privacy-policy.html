Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Last Updated: February 8, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to TourVista. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data with transparency and care. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using TourVista, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password when you register.
  • Profile Data: Profile picture, avatar, and account preferences.
  • Event Data: Registration history, payment records, wishlist items, and event participation.
  • Payment Information: Transaction IDs from bKash/Nagad payments. We do not store your mobile banking PIN or credentials.
  • Chat Data: Messages exchanged with our AI chatbot to provide better assistance.
  • OAuth Data: If you sign in with Google or GitHub, we receive your name and email from the provider.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, timestamps, and pages visited for improving our service.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your TourVista account.
  • Process event registrations and payments.
  • Send notifications about registration status updates.
  • Provide AI chatbot assistance personalized to your account.
  • Improve our platform features and user experience.
  • Communicate service updates and important announcements.
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • Payment Processors: bKash and Nagad receive necessary transaction details to process payments.
  • AI Services: Chat messages are processed by Google Gemini AI to generate helpful responses. No personally identifiable information is sent beyond the chat content.
  • OAuth Providers: Google and GitHub handle authentication; we only receive your basic profile info.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or court order.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt rounds — we never store plain-text passwords.
  • Authentication uses secure JWT tokens with expiration.
  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption.
  • File uploads are validated for type and size limits.

While we strive to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password for your account.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through your profile page.
  • Update your name, phone number, and profile picture at any time.
  • Delete your account by contacting support@tourvista.com.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (note: this may limit platform functionality).
  • Request a copy of your personal data in a readable format.

Cookies & Local Storage

TourVista uses browser local storage to:

  • Keep you logged in (storing your authentication token).
  • Remember user preferences and settings.

We do not use third-party tracking cookies. You can clear local storage through your browser settings at any time.

Children's Privacy

TourVista is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of TourVista after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
