Getting Started

1. Create Your Account

Sign up for free using your email, or quickly register with Google or GitHub. Add a profile photo and phone number to complete your profile.

2. Browse Events

Explore our collection of tours, treks, cycling trips, and outings. Use filters to find events by category, difficulty, or destination. Save your favorites to your wishlist.

3. Register & Pay

Click "Register Now" on any event, then complete payment via bKash or Nagad. Your registration will be set to Pending until an admin approves it.
